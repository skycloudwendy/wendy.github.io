Genshin Impact

About

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG developed by miHoYo. Explore the world of Teyvat, form your own party of characters, and take on a variety of enemies and challenges. With stunning graphics, engaging gameplay, and a vast world to discover, Genshin Impact is an unforgettable experience.
